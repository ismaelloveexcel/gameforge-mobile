## 🌙 Ramadan Campaign (Feb 28 - Mar 29)

### Campaign Overview

**Objective:** 3,000 total users + AED 8,000 monthly revenue  
**Budget:** $1,000 (AED 3,670)  
**Theme:** "Share Blessings Through Play"

### Cultural Considerations

**DO:**
- ✅ Post after iftar (8:30 PM UAE time)
- ✅ Use Ramadan/Eid themes
- ✅ Arabic-first approach
- ✅ Emphasize family & community
- ✅ Respectful, spiritual tone
- ✅ Focus on giving, not receiving

**DON'T:**
- ❌ Post during fasting hours
- ❌ Use overly commercial language
- ❌ Show food/drinks in content
- ❌ Rush or pressure tactics
- ❌ Western-centric messaging

### Key Messages

1. **"رمضان كريم"** (Ramadan Kareem)
   - Share blessings digitally
   - Connect with family abroad
   - Eid gift preparation

2. **"من القلب إلى القلب"** (From Heart to Heart)
   - Emotional connections
   - Family bonds
   - Spiritual joy

3. **"هدايا مميزة للعيد"** (Special Eid Gifts)
   - Prepare gifts during Ramadan
   - Send on Eid
   - Premium quality

### Ramadan Content Calendar

**Week 1 (Feb 28 - Mar 6): Introduction**
- Introduce GameForge for Ramadan
- Showcase family-friendly games
- Arabic tutorials
- Post-iftar timing

**Week 2 (Mar 7 - 13): Education**
- How to create Ramadan-themed games
- Sharing with family abroad
- Gift scheduling features

**Week 3 (Mar 14 - 20): Eid Preparation**
- Create Eid gifts now
- Premium games showcase
- Family bundle offers

**Week 4 (Mar 21 - 27): Final Push**
- Last chance for Eid gifts
- Urgency (respectfully)
- Testimonials from users

**Eid Week (Mar 28 - Apr 3): Celebration**
- Eid Mubarak messages
- Showcase user-created gifts
- Thank you content

### Ramadan-Specific Features

**Develop These:**
1. **Ramadan Theme**
   - Crescent moon aesthetics
   - Lantern graphics
   - Islamic geometric patterns
   - No characters/faces (respectful)

2. **Arabic Language**
   - Full app translation
   - Arabic game templates
   - RTL support

3. **Family Sharing**
   - Create once, send to multiple family members
   - Group gifting features
   - Family package pricing

4. **Scheduled Delivery**
   - Schedule for Eid morning
   - Automatic sending
