## Content Tiers & Monetization

### Tier 1: FREE — "Instant Gifts"
**What:** Simple, quick personalization games  
**Why Free:** User acquisition, viral sharing  
**Revenue:** None (investment in growth)

### Tier 2: FEATURED — "Seasonal Drops"
**What:** Limited-time themed games (Valentine's, Eid, etc.)  
**Why Special:** Urgency + relevance = conversion  
**Revenue:** AED 10-15 per gift

### Tier 3: PREMIUM — "Curator's Collection"
**What:** High-production-value games with deeper personalization  
**Why Premium:** Emotional impact justifies price  
**Revenue:** AED 20-35 per gift

### Tier 4: EXCLUSIVE — "Bespoke Creations"
**What:** Custom games for specific use cases (corporate, weddings)  
**Why Exclusive:** High value, low volume  
**Revenue:** AED 100-500 per game
