## 🎮 Game Framework Recommendations

### Immediate: Phaser 3 Integration (Optional)
- Better 2D performance than current Pixi.js
- Massive template library
- Mobile-first optimization
- **Timeline:** 2 weeks

### Immediate: Retro Console Templates
- TIC-80, PICO-8 aesthetics
- Lower complexity = faster generation
- Nostalgia factor for gifts
- **Timeline:** 1 week per template

### Phase 2: Matter.js Physics
- Physics-based gift games
- "Heart Stack", "Gift Toss" mechanics
- **Timeline:** 1 week per template

### Not Recommended:
- ❌ ControlNeXt, AnimateAnyone (too complex)
- ❌ Effect Games, ZQuestClassic (outdated)
- ❌ OpenMMD, JIA-Lab research (not production-ready)

## 🎓 Key Learnings from Research

### Marketing Tools
- Multi-agent AI (CrewAI) > single-purpose tools
- Instagram is THE platform for UAE gift-giving
- WhatsApp critical for direct engagement
- Automation essential for scale

### Game Frameworks
- Phaser > Pixi.js for mobile 2D
- Retro aesthetics = lower complexity + nostalgia
- Physics engines add "wow factor"
- Avoid overly complex AAA tools

### UAE Market Insights
- Valentine's Day growing trend (not traditional)
- Ramadan is MASSIVE opportunity
- 80%+ mobile engagement
- Peak times: 8 PM - 2 AM
- Arabic + English required
- Premium pricing works if quality delivers
